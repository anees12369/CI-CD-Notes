# **CI/CD Tools**

**GitLab CI/CD**

- Integrated into GitLab.

- User-friendly, seamless if you already use GitLab for version control.

**Jenkins**

- Open-source and extremely powerful.

- Highly customizable with plugins but can be complex.

**CircleCI**

- Cloud-based, fast, and simple.

- Integrates well with GitHub and Bitbucket.

- Great for small teams and startups due to quick setup.

**Travis CI**

- Cloud-based, integrates with GitHub.

- Simple and easy to get started.

- Works well out of the box for automated testing and deployment.

**GitHub Actions**

- Built directly into GitHub.

- Deep integration with GitHub projects, ideal for open-source workflows.

- Offers flexibility for automation.

- Cloud-native CI/CD services

- AWS, Azure, GCP also provide their own CI/CD tools.

**Key takeaway:**

- Each CI/CD tool has its strengths and best use cases. The choice depends on your workflow, team size, and the platforms you already use.
